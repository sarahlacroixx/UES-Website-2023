Eng Goes To Ceeps
The old bards told of a tale, an ancient tale, a mystical tale. The tale of a mass pilgrimage of drunks degenerates, and…
You should know by now, in the words of the wise Lady Godiva: “March forth to Ceeps, and thy shall receive thy beer”.
Ready your covies, boilersuits, eng shirts and jackets. Don your aviators, helmets and prepare for the ancient tradition of….
ENG GOES TO CEEPS!!!!
This event happens one time each semester, usually in November and March. The finalized dates will be announced closer to the date!
How to Sign Up/Participate:
Follow the UES Instagram account for updates on when this event is happening and how to sign up!
