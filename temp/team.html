VP Academic
Jack Peplinski

About Me:
Hey there! My name's Jack Peplinski and I'm super excited to be your VP Academic! I'm originally from Calgary (s/o Stampede), have two older sisters, and a dog named Finn. I'm doing the software engineering and business dual degree. You've probably heard that first year can be hard. My first year was not an exception. Managing your health, making friends, moving away from home for many of you, and managing school on top of all that can be hard. I failed a few assignments and even some major exams, applied for tons of jobs and scholarships I didn't get, and had many more failures. I'm happy to do what I can to help you. Please do not hesitate to reach out to me via email (jpeplin2@uwo.ca), phone (403-991-6634), Messenger, or carrier pigeon. Take deep breaths, go out with friends, and study hard! Engineering is a team sport.
